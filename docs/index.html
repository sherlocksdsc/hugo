Sherlock at Work

The experts at Sherlock have developed and deployed specific cloud services designed to provide a solid and secure foundation for a wide range of initiatives. The success and impact of Sherlock has been resounding, as it has already helped support a number of critical applications including Fighting Medicaid Fraud, Cancer Research, Enterprise Risk Management, Healthcare Research and more. Further, Sherlock has been recognized by the University of California Office of the President for Information Technology Innovation through its Sautter Award Program.

About us

Sherlock, an offering of SDSC's Health Cyberinfrastructure Division, is a Center of Excellence focused on managed information technology, compliance, and data services for academia and government that spans many IT disciplines, including compliant cloud hosting, cloud security, and data management.

News & Events

Sherlock is based at the San Diego Supercomputer (SDSC) on the University of California, San Diego campus. For the latest Sherlock press releases, news, and events, please visit the SDSC News & Events page.
